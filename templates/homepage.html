Any Questions? Answered

There are many Questions? You want to ask as about are application. Before you start read few of them.

You only need to fill out a simple form and decide which position you are registering for. The position can be owner or worker depending on the service you need. You can apply for both positions, you just need to register with two different emails and username.

If you are registered as an owner, you have the option of placing an ad on our page. The ad must contain the basic information found in the form. After the announcement is finished, you can change your ad as needed and wait for the workers to contact you to make a deal.

If you are registered as an Worker, you can view all ads by the owner and send a message directly to the owner related to the ad they posted.

Only users who are registered as owners can post ads. The ad can be changed depending on the owner's request. Ads cannot be placed by users who are registered as

Users who are registered as workers have the possibility to send a message to the ad owner first, in order to make their offer for the ad. The ability to send the first message is only available to worker users.
